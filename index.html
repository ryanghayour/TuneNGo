Tune 'N Go

Who We Are

We're a team of lifelong skiers who want to provide

a cheap, accessible alternaative to tuning skis, so that

your skis stay fresh all day long.

What We Do

Whether you're taking a quick hot chocolate break with your

friends or meeting the family to eat lunch, we'll take your

skis off your hands and tune them right there in the lodge at

one of our certified tuning booths. By the time you're done,

your skis will feel brand new as they're handed to you out the door.

Why Tune Skis?

Have you ever tried to make a turn on that classic east coast ice

and your skis just can't catch a grip? Our tuning professionals will

make sure that your skis cut right through that frozen terrain.

And, for a bit extra, we'll wax your skis and make them faster

than you've ever seen them!
